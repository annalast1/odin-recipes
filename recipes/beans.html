Quick and Easy Baked Beans

Ingredients

  • Â½ tsp rapeseed oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely grated
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 400g can cannellini beans

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a small non-stick pan and fry the garlic over a low heat for about a minute, stirring to soften.
  2. Add the tomato puree, vinegar and paprika, and cook, stirring, for about a minute more.
  3. Tip in the beans and the liquid from the can. Cook for a few minutes so the beans are coated in the sauce, then serve.
